Why Cloud Computing Matters

Whether you're migrating to Amazon Web Services (AWS), building on Microsoft Azure, or leveraging Google Cloud Platform (GCP), choosing the right cloud strategy is critical for success.

DevOps is more than just a buzzword‚Äîit's a cultural shift that brings development and operations together to deliver software faster and more reliably. MLOps: Operationalizing Machine Learning

MLOps bridges the gap between machine learning development and production deployment. With MLOps, organizations can deploy models faster, monitor performance in real-time, and maintain model quality over time. I specialize in building ML pipelines, implementing model monitoring, and establishing ML governance frameworks.

AIOps: AI-Powered IT Operations

AIOps uses artificial intelligence and machine learning to enhance IT operations. By implementing AIOps solutions, organizations can predict incidents before they occur, automate remediation, and significantly reduce mean time to resolution (MTTR). My AIOps expertise includes anomaly detection, intelligent alerting, and automated incident response.

Model Context Protocol (MCP) & AI Agents

The Model Context Protocol (MCP) represents the next evolution in AI systems, enabling seamless communication between AI agents and tools.
