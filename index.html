Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Who are these courses for?

My courses are designed for engineers with at least basic programming knowledge who want to transition into DevOps, Cloud Engineering, or AI/ML Operations. Whether you're a developer, sysadmin, or recent graduate, if you're serious about building production systems, you're in the right place.

What makes your training different?

Unlike tutorial-based courses, my training is project-driven and mirrors real enterprise scenarios. You'll work on actual production-grade projects, not toy examples. Every assignment is reviewed personally, and you'll receive detailed feedback just like you would in a real job.

Do I need prior experience?

Basic programming knowledge and understanding of Linux are recommended. However, I provide prerequisite materials for students who need to level up their fundamentals. The key requirement is dedication and willingness to learn.

How long are the courses?

Courses range from 8 to 16 weeks depending on the program. Each course includes weekly live sessions, hands-on labs, projects, and access to recorded materials for lifetime learning.

Will I get job assistance?

Yes! All students get resume reviews, mock interviews, and career guidance. I also connect top performers with companies in my network actively hiring for cloud and DevOps roles.

What if I can't attend live sessions?

All sessions are recorded and available for later viewing. However, I strongly encourage live attendance for interactive learning and immediate doubt resolution.
