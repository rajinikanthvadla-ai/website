FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who are these courses for?

Engineers with basic programming knowledge who want to transition into DevOps, Cloud Engineering, or AI/ML Operations. Developers, sysadmins, and graduates are all welcome.

What makes your training different?

My training is project-driven and mirrors real enterprise scenarios. You'll work on production-grade projects with personal code reviews and feedback.

Do I need prior experience?

Basic programming and Linux knowledge is recommended. I provide prerequisite materials for those who need to level up their fundamentals.

How long are the courses?

Courses range from 8 to 16 weeks. Each includes weekly live sessions, hands-on labs, projects, and lifetime access to recordings.

Will I get job assistance?

Yes! All students receive resume reviews, mock interviews, and career guidance. Top performers get connected to companies in my network.

What if I can't attend live sessions?

All sessions are recorded and available for later viewing. However, live attendance is encouraged for interactive learning.
